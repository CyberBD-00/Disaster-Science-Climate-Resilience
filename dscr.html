About the Program

Abstract

This document presents the full, course-specific curriculum for the four-year (8-semester, 152-credit) BSc in Disaster Science & Climate Resilience. Each course includes tailored objectives, detailed contents, learning outcomes, and recommended references to ensure alignment with the distinct skills and competencies required across climatology, hydrology, geosciences, geoinformatics, risk reduction, and disaster management.

Introduction

The curriculum develops progressive capability—from foundational science to advanced modelling, hazard monitoring, risk-sensitive planning, and capstone research. Course design follows constructive alignment principles, ensuring that learning outcomes are directly assessable through labs, fieldwork, projects, and examinations.
